Our History

OceanOne Beach Hotel, established in the early 1980s, began as a modest seaside retreat aimed at providing a tranquil escape for visitors. Located on a pristine stretch of coastline, the hotel quickly became renowned for its stunning ocean views and exceptional hospitality. Over the decades, OceanOne underwent several expansions and renovations to accommodate a growing number of guests and to upgrade its amenities. The hotel has maintained its charm and elegance, blending modern conveniences with classic beachfront appeal. Today, OceanOne Beach Hotel is celebrated as a premier destination for both leisure and business travelers seeking a luxurious coastal experience.

Our Mission

At OceanOne Beach Hotel, our mission is to provide an unparalleled coastal retreat where luxury meets tranquility. We are dedicated to offering exceptional hospitality, personalized service, and unique experiences that celebrate the natural beauty of our beachfront location. Our commitment is to create a serene and rejuvenating environment, ensuring every guest leaves with cherished memories and a longing to return
